Welcome to our FYP Project

Pakistan has a great future in mining because it has huge deposits of minerals. Rate of mining accidents increases with an increase in production. Mines in Pakistan are referred as “Death traps” and “Black Mountains”

Monitoring Miners

Track the location of workers inside the mines. Provide real time footage for the surveillance of miners' activity.

Smart Safety Helmet

Smart safety helmet which will detect and monitor air quality, temperature, humidity, and hazardous gases

Web-Based Application

A web-based application which will display and monitor the location and data received from the sensors.

About our project

To create an IoT-based safety helmet to detect hazardous gases around the miners and for real time tracking of miners using RFID.
To develop a web-based application in order to monitor the activity and conditions of mines and miners
